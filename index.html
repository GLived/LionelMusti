Welcome to Lionel Musti!

The tiny dog with incredible dribbling skills

Who is Lionel Musti?

Lionel Musti is a nickname given to a 16 month old Jack Russell Terrier named Mustikas "Musti". She has always loved playing outside, and one day she found an old basketball lying around.
Lacking opposable thumbs, she did what she does best now. Started dribbling the ball with her snoot and she's never looked back.

Origins of Lionel Musti

Mustikas was born on a small farm as a 5th generation JRT. She was adopted my an Eastern European and South American. When her football passion and skills grew, she needed a name to represent it.
Lionel Messi is the undisputed GOAT of football, so it was only fitting the she would be known as Leonel Musti.
